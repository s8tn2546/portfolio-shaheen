About Me

I am a passionate and creative fashion designer with a strong foundation in design principles, garment construction, and trend forecasting. As a recent graduate in Fashion Design, I bring fresh perspectives, a keen eye for detail, and a deep appreciation for aesthetic innovation. I specialize in combining traditional craftsmanship with modern silhouettes to create designs that are both stylish and wearable. From sketching ideas to bringing them to life through fabric and form, I enjoy every stage of the design process. I am skilled in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and CLO3D, and I have hands-on experience in pattern making, draping, and fabric selection. My design philosophy revolves around individuality, sustainability, and storytelling through fashion. Eager to learn and grow, I’m looking for opportunities to contribute to dynamic design teams where I can apply my creativity, adaptability, and strong work ethic to real-world fashion projects.
